Recycling Collection Schedule

Recycling pickup is every other Friday. Residents are asked to be sure to have the carts at the curb by 7AM on their collection days.

Recycling Programs

The The town will pick up yard waste from town residents at the curb on Wednesdays, Thursday afternoons and Fridays. Recycling pickup is on the same day as trash pickup. Town residents may use the County LCID site as needed.
